Our Services

Structural Design Consultancy

SCMCES supports clients in making informed decisions on how structures are designed and reviewed. We ensure that buildings and infrastructure are not only code-compliant, but also practical, safe, and suited to their intended use throughout their service life.

SCMCES offers expert structural analysis and design consultancy for buildings, bridges, industrial structures, and complex civil infrastructure. Our services include design review, code compliance checks, performance-based analysis, and constructability assessment, ensuring safe, economical, and durable structural solutions aligned with Indian and international standards.

Remedial & Rehabilitation Engineering

When structures show signs of distress, damage, or performance issues, SCMCES helps identify what is really going wrong and why.

Based on detailed investigation, testing, and analysis, we recommend targeted repair and strengthening solutions that improve safety and extend the usable life of the structure.

SCMCES provides advanced structural assessment, repair, and rehabilitation solutions for distressed and aging structures using Non-Destructive Testing (NDT), load testing, and root-cause evaluation.

Our retrofitting, strengthening, and vibration mitigation solutions are based on Advanced Rehabilitation Technologies (ART) to restore performance and extend service life.

Structural Monitoring & SHM Services

SCMCES helps clients see how their structures actually behave under real operating conditions. By measuring vibration, strain, and movement, we provide clear engineering insights that support maintenance planning, risk reduction, and long-term infrastructure reliability.

SCMCES delivers comprehensive Structural Monitoring and Structural Health Monitoring (SHM) services for civil infrastructure subjected to static and dynamic loads. Our solutions include sensor-based monitoring, vibration assessment, data analysis, and condition evaluation, supporting construction-stage monitoring, operational safety, and long-term asset management.

Education, Training & Research Support

Alongside consultancy services, SCMCES actively supports the civil engineering community through training programs, internships, and research guidance in areas such as structural analysis & design review, structural assessment, NDT, load testing, vibration studies, SHM, and remedial engineering—helping engineers bridge theory with real-world practice.
