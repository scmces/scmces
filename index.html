SCM Consultation & Engineering Services (SCMCES)

SCMCES provides consultancy and engineering services for civil infrastructure. In addition to professional services, SCMCES also provides training, internship, and research support for civil engineers in areas related to structural analysis & design review, Structural Assessment, Non-Destructive Testing (NDT), Static/Dynamic load testing, Vibration assessment, Structural Health Monitoring (SHM), and remedial engineering using Advanced Rehabilitation Technologies (ART).
